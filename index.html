What is Agile

In plain words, Agile mindset is the ability to create and respond to change.
For project management in software development, Agile methodology is a set of processes through which requirements and solutions evolve through the collaborative effort of self-organising and cross-functional teams and their customer(s)/end user(s)

Agile Manifesto

The creators of the agile methodology have signed and published a manifesto that acts as guidance for all teams using Agile to develop, deliver and sustain software products across the globe.
The agile manifesto: Individuals and interactions over processes and tools
Working software over comprehensive documentation
Customer collaboration over contract negotiation
Responding to change over following a plan
